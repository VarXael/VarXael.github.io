About Me

I design games where the systems tell the story.

I’m a game designer who thinks systematically — I architect interconnected mechanics where every piece has a reason to exist, and where the design and the experience are inseparable. From a music-driven event framework to a game where scanning IS the entire gameplay loop, my work is about building systems that create meaning.

Everything I design, I can build. I work in Unreal Engine and C++, and I move fluently between design documentation and implementation. When I’m on a team, I tend to become the person who brings clarity to the vision and structure to the process — not because I claim the role, but because it happens naturally through how I communicate and work.

I’ve ported shipped titles to Nintendo Switch, led teams from concept to release, won awards for my university work, and I’m currently building a game where the world doesn’t exist until you scan it into memory.
